FAQ

Common
questions.

Everything you need to know before getting started with LMCT PRO.

How long is the free trial?↓
The 14-day free trial is available on the Software plan only — no credit card required. The Managed and Growth plans include your assigned specialist and website build which start from day one, so there is no free trial period on those plans. You can start on the Software trial and upgrade to Managed or Growth anytime.
Is there a lock-in contract?↓
No. All plans are month-to-month. Cancel anytime with no penalty and no notice period. We earn your business every single month — that's our commitment to you.
What does the "under 60 minutes" guarantee mean?↓
When you send your specialist photos and vehicle details during business hours (Mon–Fri, 9am–6pm), they will have your car live on CarSales, Gumtree, Facebook Marketplace, and your website in under 60 minutes. Our average is 38 minutes.
What is included in the dealer website?↓
A professionally designed, SEO-optimised website that auto-syncs with your LMCT PRO stock. Add a car and it appears on your site. Sell it and it disappears. One-time build — yours forever, no ongoing website fees. Growth plan dealers receive premium design and monthly improvement updates.
How do I send new cars to my specialist?↓
WhatsApp is the easiest — send photos and basic details (make, model, year, km, rego, price). Your specialist handles everything in under 60 minutes. No app knowledge required from you.
Does LMCT PRO work outside Victoria?↓
The platform works nationally. Compliance form auto-fill is built for Victoria. NSW and QLD compliance forms are on the roadmap — speak to us about your state's specific requirements.
Does it sync with Xero or MYOB?↓
Yes, both. Two-way sync with Xero and MYOB included on all plans. GST calculations for motor vehicle transactions are handled automatically in line with ATO requirements for licensed motor car traders.
